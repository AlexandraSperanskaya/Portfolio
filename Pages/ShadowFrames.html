Shadow Frames

Shadow Frames is a short horror game made by a team of 6 people, including me. As one of the two programmers, I handled the UI, half of the gameplay progamming, localisation, saving/loading functionality, version control (supporting team members, handling weekly final builds and commits to master, running the project on machines that were not used to work on it); the other programmer, Ine Hoecedez, the enemy AI, the other half of the gameplay programming, and level streaming.

A semester long-project, we were working on this for our Group Porject course, but we are going to continue working on this in the future in order to expand and improve on things we did not have time for during the semester, to fulfil our vision on this game. An itch.io page will be up by the end of April, and we will be documenting and uploading new builds as we work on finishing it.

I was working hybrid, with 4 in person hours and 28 remote hours, and the rest of the team was working in-person.
